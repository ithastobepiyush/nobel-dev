About Us

Oraios AI — derived from the Greek word for “beautiful” — was founded in 2024 by friends and long-time collaborators Dominik Jain and Michael Panchenko. Our goal: to launch an independent venture dedicated to delivering genuine value to customers through deep AI expertise and operational efficiency.

With decades of collective in-house experience across demanding projects, industries, and domains, we don’t just build systems — we build the right ones. Our strength lies in combining engineering rigor with efficiency, delivering solutions that are robust, elegantly designed, and built to last.

As AI reshapes how software is built, we apply our experience to bridge the gap between potential and practice. We collaborate closely with clients to accelerate development, improve quality, and uncover new opportunities for innovation — always driven by a commitment to technical excellence and real-world impact.
